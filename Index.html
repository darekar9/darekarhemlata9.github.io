THE AREEJ STORY

Born from the ancient souks of Arabia, Areej (أريج) means "the fragrance that fills the air." For generations, we have sourced the finest oud wood from the forests of Cambodia, India, and Borneo — hand-selected by master perfumers to create scents that tell stories of desert nights, royal palaces, and timeless traditions.
