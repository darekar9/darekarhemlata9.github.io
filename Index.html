ABOUT AREEJ

عن أريج

A PASSION BORN IN DOHA

My name is Hemlata Darekar, and my love for Arabian fragrances was born the moment I arrived in the golden city of Doha, Qatar. The warm desert air, the ancient souks filled with rising wisps of oud smoke, the elegance of Arabic culture — it all captured my heart completely.

I spent years wandering the perfume markets of Doha, learning from master perfumers, understanding the sacred art of oud — the wood that Arabia calls "liquid gold." Every fragrance I encountered told a story of heritage, royalty, and timeless beauty.

That passion became Areej — which means "the beautiful fragrance that fills the air." I created Areej to bring the soul of Arabia to the world, through handcrafted oud perfumes that honour tradition while embracing modern luxury. Every bottle is a piece of Doha, crafted with love.

— Hemlata Darekar, Founder of Areej

THE AREEJ STORY

Born from the ancient souks of Arabia, Areej (أريج) means "the fragrance that fills the air." We source the finest oud wood from the forests of Cambodia, India, and Borneo — hand-selected by master perfumers to create scents that tell stories of desert nights, royal palaces, and timeless Arabian traditions. Every drop of Areej carries the warmth of Doha and the soul of Arabia.
